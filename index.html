    Ahmad Mousavi, Ph.D.

    I am a Postdoctoral Associate at the Informatics Institute at the University of Florida. I completed my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County under the guidance of Professor Jinglai Shen in 2019. My research interests include sparse optimization, machine learning, large-scale optimization, mathematical modeling, and numerical linear algebra.

    My work focuses on:

    • Sparse Optimization: L1-minimization, constrained matching pursuit, greedy algorithms
    • Machine Learning: Support vector machines, quadratic surface classifiers, large-scale optimization
    • Mathematical Modeling: Semidefinite programming, matrix decomposition, numerical linear algebra
